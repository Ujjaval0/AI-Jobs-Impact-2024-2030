## 📊 Overview

- **30,000+ Job Titles** — Employment trends across 8 industries and 5 global regions

---

## 🎯 Key Insights

### 💰 Startups & ROI
- **AI ROI is lowest** → $7.90 per $1 invested (vs. E-Commerce: $10.00)
- **76% of startups never exit** → IPO/acquisition is the exception, not the norm
- **Geography no longer matters** → All 5 regions within 2% of each other in funding share

### 💵 Funding Trends (2024-2025)
- **India leads in deal size** → $745M average (2x USA's $403M)
- **60% of funding bypasses AI** → Largest deals: SpaceX ($1.5B), Byju's ($1.2B)
- **FinTech is underrated** → $2,425M raised with minimal media coverage (nearly matching pure AI)

### 💼 Job Market by 2030
| Metric | Winner | Loser |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Job Growth** | IT: +511,824 jobs | Transportation: -444,000 jobs |
| **Avg. Salary** | IT: $90,942/year | Transportation: $89,451/year |
| **Automation Risk** | Safest roles need human judgment | 4,437 titles already shrinking (70%+ risk) |

**Reality Check:** $1,491 salary gap won't protect your career — skills matter more than pay.

## 📌 Actionable Recommendations

### For Job Seekers & Students
✅ **Pursue IT or Healthcare** — Best combo of growth + pay + automation resilience  
✅ **Build human skills** — Judgment, creativity, problem-solving beat certifications  
✅ **Stay flexible** — Roles requiring physical presence or complex thinking are safest  

### For Business Leaders
✅ **Don't chase AI hype** — ROI data favors E-Commerce and HealthTech  
✅ **Rethink operations** — Transportation and Education sectors need urgent restructuring  
✅ **Focus on fundamentals** — Sustainable returns > trending buzzwords
